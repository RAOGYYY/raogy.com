By using Paani Boss, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

This app collects location data to enable 'Field Staff Tracking' and 'Navigation' even when the app is closed or not in use.

We collect precise location data from technicians/field staff for the following purposes:

  • Real-time tracking: Shop owners can monitor field staff locations during working hours
  • Route optimization: Navigate technicians to customer locations efficiently
  • Service verification: Confirm technician presence at service locations
  • Background tracking: Location tracking continues when the app is minimized or closed to maintain accurate field staff monitoring

  • Invoice/receipt uploads: Attach proof of purchase for parts and materials

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Provide core app functionality for business management
  • Field Staff Management: Track technician locations, assign tasks, monitor service completion
  • Customer Service: Maintain customer records, service history, and AMC schedules
  • Communication: Send service reminders, payment alerts, and app notifications
  • Analytics: Improve app performance and user experience
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and security incidents

5. Data Security

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
