Privacy Policy

ScreenScribe - Offline Screenshot Manager

100% Offline No Data Collection Last Updated: March 25, 2026

✓ Complete Privacy Guarantee

ScreenScribe does NOT have internet permission. The app literally cannot send any data anywhere. All processing happens 100% on your device.

1 Introduction

Welcome to ScreenScribe ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the ScreenScribe mobile application (the "App").

ScreenScribe is a 100% offline screenshot management application that uses on-device OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract and organize text from your screenshots. The app is designed with privacy as its core principle.

Key Point: ScreenScribe does not include the INTERNET permission in its Android manifest. This means the app is technically incapable of connecting to the internet or transmitting any data.

2 Data We Do NOT Collect

Unlike most apps, ScreenScribe collects absolutely no data from you. Here's what we explicitly do NOT do:

No Personal Information: We don't collect names, emails, or any identifiers
No Screenshot Data: Your screenshots never leave your device
No OCR Text: Extracted text stays in local database only
No Analytics: No usage tracking, no telemetry, no metrics
No Crash Reports: No automatic error reporting to servers
No Location Data: We don't access your location
No Advertising IDs: No ad tracking whatsoever

3 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

ScreenScribe stores the following data locally on your device only in an encrypted SQLite database:

Data Type Purpose Storage
Screenshot metadata File path, date, category Local Room DB
Extracted OCR text Search and organization Local Room DB
User notes & tags Personal organization Local Room DB
Transaction records Financial tracking Local Room DB
App preferences Settings & customization DataStore

All this data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

4 App Permissions

ScreenScribe requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

📁 Storage Access (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES)

Required to read screenshot images from your device storage for OCR processing.

🔔 Notification Access (Optional)

Optional permission to read banking notifications for automatic transaction detection. You can deny this permission.

🔐 Biometric (USE_BIOMETRIC)

Optional permission for fingerprint/face unlock to secure the app and vault.

Note: We explicitly do NOT request INTERNET permission. This is intentional and ensures your data cannot be transmitted.

5 On-Device OCR Processing

ScreenScribe uses Google ML Kit Text Recognition V2 for OCR. Here's how it works:

  • ML Kit models are downloaded once via Google Play Services
  • All text recognition runs entirely on your device
  • No images or text are sent to Google or any server
  • Supports Latin, Devanagari (Hindi), Chinese, Japanese, and Korean scripts

Google's ML Kit on-device processing is designed for privacy. Learn more at ML Kit Documentation.

6 Data Security

Your data is protected by multiple layers of security:

🔒 App Lock

Biometric or passcode protection for app access

🗄️ Secret Vault

Additional protection for sensitive screenshots

🗑️ Auto-Delete

Automatic deletion of sensitive data (OTPs, passwords)

📱 Device Security

Data protected by your device's built-in encryption

7 Your Data Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

Access: View all your data anytime within the app

Export: Export your data from Settings

Delete: Clear all data from Settings → Clear Data

Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes all app data

Note: Since we don't have your data on any server, there's nothing for us to delete. All deletion happens locally on your device.

8 Children's Privacy

ScreenScribe does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since the app works entirely offline and stores data only on the user's device, there are no COPPA compliance concerns.
