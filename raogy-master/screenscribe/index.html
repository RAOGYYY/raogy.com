Powerful Features

Everything you need to manage, search, and extract value from your screenshots

Multi-Script OCR

Extract text in English, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean. Powered by Google ML Kit running entirely on-device.

AI Auto-Categorization

Screenshots automatically sorted into Bills, Shopping, Travel, Social Media, Memes, Health, Education, and more.

Transaction Tracking

Auto-detect UPI payments, bank transactions, and expenses from screenshots. Monthly reports and budget tracking.

QR Code Scanner

Automatically detect QR codes and barcodes. Quick actions for URLs, UPI payments, WiFi, phone numbers, and emails.

Instant Search

FTS4 full-text search finds any screenshot by its content in milliseconds. Search through thousands of screenshots instantly.

Secret Vault

Hide sensitive screenshots behind biometric or passcode lock. Vault screenshots are completely hidden from main view.

Quick Copy Chips

One-tap copy for amounts, emails, phone numbers, UPI IDs, OTPs, and URLs extracted from screenshots.

Smart Folders

Auto-organized folders by source app, sensitive content, amounts, contacts, and more. Find what you need instantly.

Due Date Detection

Auto-detect bill due dates from screenshots. Get reminders and add directly to your calendar with one tap.
