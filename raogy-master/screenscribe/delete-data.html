Data Deletion Instructions for ScreenScribe

100% Offline App No Server Data

Good News: Your Data Never Left Your Device!

ScreenScribe is a 100% offline application that does not have internet permission. This means:

  • We have no servers storing your data
  • Your screenshots and extracted text never leave your device
  • There is no account to delete
  • All data deletion happens locally on your phone

1 Delete Data from Within the App

The easiest way to delete your data while keeping the app installed:

1

Open ScreenScribe

Launch the app on your device

2

Go to Settings

Tap the Settings icon (⚙️) in the app

3

Tap "Clear Data"

Scroll down to find the "Clear Data" option

4

Confirm Deletion

Confirm when prompted. This will delete all app data including:

  • All screenshot metadata and OCR text
  • All personal notes and tags
  • All transaction records
  • All app preferences
  • Vault contents

Note: This does NOT delete your actual screenshot image files. Those remain in your device's gallery/storage.

2 Complete Deletion by Uninstalling

To completely remove all ScreenScribe data from your device:

1

Long-press the App Icon

Find ScreenScribe on your home screen or app drawer

2

Select "Uninstall"

Or go to Settings → Apps → ScreenScribe → Uninstall

3

Confirm Uninstallation

This permanently removes the app and ALL its data

Complete Removal: Uninstalling the app removes ALL ScreenScribe data from your device. There is nothing left behind.

3 Clear Data via Android Settings

You can also clear app data through Android system settings:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Apps or Applications
  3. Find and tap ScreenScribe
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear Data and confirm

4 What Gets Deleted

DELETED

  • Screenshot metadata & categories
  • Extracted OCR text
  • Personal notes & tags
  • Transaction records
  • Vault contents
  • App preferences & settings

NOT DELETED

  • Your actual screenshot images
  • Files in your device gallery

Screenshot images are stored in your device's storage, not within the app. Delete them manually from your gallery if needed.

5 No Server-Side Data to Delete

Unlike most apps, ScreenScribe does not have:

  • No user accounts — Nothing to delete on our end
  • No cloud storage — Your data was never uploaded
  • No analytics data — We don't track anything
  • No backup servers — Everything is local only

This means: Once you delete data from your device (using methods above), it's completely gone. There's no "request data deletion" process needed because we never had your data in the first place.
