4 Coins & Redemptions

Virtual Coins: RewardNest uses a virtual coin system. Coins are earned through in-app activities (spinning, quizzes, scratch cards, referrals). Coins have no real-world monetary value by themselves and cannot be exchanged for cash directly.

Redemption requests are processed manually by our team within 7–14 business days and are subject to verification. We reserve the right to reject redemption requests that appear fraudulent or violate our Terms of Service.
